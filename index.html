Biography

I am Xiwei Deng, or Albert Darren, a master's student at the Institute of Artificial Intelligence and Fujian Provincial Key Laboratory of Plasma and Magnetic Resonance at Xiamen University. Currently, my research focuses on automatically generating a natural language report from medical images, constructing more comprehensive datasets, and further developing a CAD system based on the medical image report generation algorithm. My favorite motto from high school to now is, “The best way to predict the future is to create it.”–Alan Kay, GPU pioneer and computer scientist.
